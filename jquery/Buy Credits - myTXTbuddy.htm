Top up with iWallet

This is the simplest way to top-up your MTB account. If you do not yet have an account on iWallet, sign up (http://www.iwallet.com.gh), deposit money in your account and go ahead to top up your MTB account. It's really easy.
  1. Log in to your account
  2. Click the recharge button at the top right corner
  3. Choose iWallet for payment
  4. Choose your preferred amount and click pay with iWallet
  5. You’ll be redirected to iWallet to pay
  6. Pay by clicking click to pay after you’ve provided your iWallet username and password
  7. Confirm your payment by clicking confirm
  8. iWallet will process the payment and redirect back to your myTXTbuddy account in 5seconds
  9. You have successfully topped up your account. Hurray!!!
